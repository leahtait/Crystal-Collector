Crystal Collector Game


Welcome to the Crystal Collecting Game!

You will be given a random number at the start of each game. There are four crystals below. Click on a crystal to add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number given to you at the start of the game,and you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number. The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it. For each new game, the value of each crystal will change!
